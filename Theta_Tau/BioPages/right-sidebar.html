Lambda Beta visits Tuscaloosa at the 2015 Theta Tau Regional Conference

February 27th

2015 Regionals

It's that time again for the latest update of our Lambda Beta crew. Just recently eight brothers returned from their trip to Tuscaloosa. The conference was held on the beautiful campus of the University of Alabama which was hosted by their own Mu chapter of Theta Tau. It was here that the companions were able to meet with other brothers from all over the country including grand regent, Brandon Satterwhite. Not only was the trip a great experience for those who attended but it was also a start to help gain ideas for the Lambda Beta chapter. We can only guess what new ideas come to fruition later in the year.

That's all the news we have for now. Standby until the next update
