What we do?

We work to stimulate professional activity and social compatibility; provide a framework for group participation on campus, community, engineering, and fraternity affairs; and promote lasting friendships - a lifetime of brotherhood in an engineering environment.

What is Theta Tau?

Theta Tau is the only co-ed professional engineering fraternity at tennessee tech, and is also the oldest fraternity on campus. founded in 1904, theta tau is the nation's oldest fraternity for engineers with chapters at over 40 universities. theta tau is open to all engineering majors on campus as well as mit.

Lambda Beta? What's that?

Lambda Beta is the oldest fraternity chapter at Tennessee Technological University. Our chapter stresses the importance of high professional ethics and exemplary practices.
